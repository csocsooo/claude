## OPTIMIZATION CHECKLIST

- **Hook (0:00–0:01.5):** "Breaking news. A man just lost a thousand aura points." → curiosity + number + stakes. No throat-clearing.
- **Pattern interrupt at 0:03:** cut to b-roll before the brain can swipe.
- **Retention loop:** the "Aura Market — LIVE" graphic forces a re-watch to read the numbers.
- **Payoff at 0:28:** signoff doubles as a CTA without sounding like a CTA.
- **Hard cut to black + on-screen "+1,000 AURA for following":** turns the follow into a bit, not a beg.
- **Audio:** dry, no music under the anchor. Add a single subtle news-sting on the lower-third graphic (0:11).
- **Subtitles:** burned-in, top third (TikTok's UI eats the bottom).
- **Aspect:** 9:16, safe zones respected — keep the desk/logo above 200px from the bottom.
